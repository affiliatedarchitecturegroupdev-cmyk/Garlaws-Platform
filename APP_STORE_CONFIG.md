# Garlaws Property Maintenance App

## iOS App Store Information

### App Name
Garlaws - Property Maintenance

### Subtitle
Smart Property Care Solutions

### Description
Transform your property maintenance experience with Garlaws, South Africa's premier property lifecycle management platform. Connect with verified service providers, book maintenance services, and manage your properties with ease.

**Key Features:**
- 🏠 **Property Management**: Comprehensive lifecycle tracking
- 📅 **Smart Booking**: AI-powered scheduling and conflict resolution
- 💬 **Real-time Chat**: Instant communication with service providers
- 📹 **Video Calls**: Virtual consultations and site inspections
- 🔔 **Push Notifications**: Stay updated on service status
- 📱 **Offline Access**: Work without internet connectivity
- 📊 **Analytics Dashboard**: Performance insights and reporting

**Enterprise Features:**
- B-BBEE Level 1 Compliance tracking
- SARS tax integration and reconciliations
- ESG reporting and sustainability metrics
- Multi-tenant architecture support
